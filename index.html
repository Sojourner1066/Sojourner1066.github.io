Union Cycliste International (UCI) International Championship Tour

An experiment with visual hierarchy to highlight a European cycling route

Click to see full resolution image




The goal of this cartograph exercise was to prepare a promotional map for the newly created International Championship Tour run by Union Cycliste International (UCI). The tour will be completed in 21 day-long stages, with a day break every 7 stages. The route will cover approximately 100 miles (160 kilometers) per day, varying in length with the terrain; there must be variation in terrain across the route to include flat and mountainous stages. The route can be discontinuous and the segments must start and end at populated places.
